Mission Statement

Rex's Home exists to rescue, foster, rehabilitate, and rehome neglected, surrendered, and misunderstood reptiles — our “modern dinosaurs.” We are dedicated to educating the public on proper reptile care, promoting responsible ownership, and giving every animal a safe, healthy second chance at life.
